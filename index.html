All about band instruments! 🎼

Discover all the instruments band has to offer!

🍃 Woodwinds

🎶

Flute

A high-pitched, agile instrument that produces sound by blowing across a hole. Fun fact, I played flute for 4 years!

🎷

Clarinet

A versatile single-reed instrument with a rich, dark lower register and bright upper notes. Prone to squeaking!!

🎷

Saxophone

Made of brass but uses a single reed. Known for its smooth, jazzy tone and expressive sound.

🎺 Brass

🎺

Trumpet

The highest brass instrument, known for its bright, triumphant, and piercing sound.

🌟

Euphonium

The best brass instrument ever! Like ever! Not biased but it's the best everr!

🎼

Trombone

Uses a slide instead of valves to change pitch, creating smooth glissandos and deep tones.

🥁 Percussion

🥁

Snare Drum

Has metal wires (snares) underneath that create a sharp, staccato "crack" when struck.

🎶

Timpani

Large copper kettle drums that can be tuned to specific pitches. They provide the rumbling foundation.

🎹

Xylophone

A mallet instrument with wooden bars, producing bright, sharp, and playful melodies.
